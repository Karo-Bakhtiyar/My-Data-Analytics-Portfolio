High-Level Sales Analysis

This part of the project focuses on creating baseline sales metrics for Balanced Tree Clothing Co. It provides the company with a clear overview of their overall performance before diving deeper into customer or product-level insights.

Key goals

Measure total sales quantities and overall revenue

Assess the impact of discounts on sales

Establish core KPIs (quantities, revenue, average selling price, discount % etc.) for reporting
